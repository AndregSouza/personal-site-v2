About André Souza

I’m a Brazilian designer focused on product strategy, user research, and creative user experience that makes a difference in people’s lives.

With more than 5 years of experience, I currently work at Pagar.me (Stone.Co) as a Senior Product Designer. Previously, I was a UX Designer and Researcher at Try (WPP), one of Brazil’s most respected UX and Research companies.

My work thrives in collaboration with product, engineering, and business analysts to drive customer-centered decisions into design solutions.

I have a genuine curiosity about people and their behaviors, always aiming to understand the “why” behind their actions and reactions through qualitative and quantitative feedback.

Outside of work, I play competitive games like League of Legends or Dota 2, watch animes and mangas, or listen to old songs remixed as lo-fi.
